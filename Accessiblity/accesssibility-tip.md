## Aria-haspopup

|||
|---|---|
|`Aria-haspopup=”false"`|the element does not have any popup|
|`Aria-haspopup=”true”`|popup is menu|
|`Aria-haspopup=”menu”`|popup is menu|
|`Aria-haspopup=”dialog”`|popup is dialog|
|`Aria-haspopup=”grid”`|popup is grid|
|`Aria-haspopup=”listbox”`|popup is listbox|
|`Aria-haspopup=”tree”`|popup is true|
